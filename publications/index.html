2025

  1. TipsRO
    PrecisionPro fusion: Clinical validation of an automated MRI-CT Fusion system for prostate radiotherapy planning
    Deondre Do, Christopher Conlin, Madison Baxter, and 8 more authors
    Technical Innovations & Patient Support in Radiation Oncology, 2025
    Abs HTML PDF

    We developed and clinically validated PrecisionPro Fusion, an automated pipeline for MRI-CT image registration in prostate radiotherapy. By replacing manual, error-prone alignment processes with a Python/MATLAB-based framework, we reduced processing time to under 15 minutes while maintaining sub-millimeter accuracy. This system facilitates the clinical deployment of focal radiation therapy boosts by ensuring high-precision targeting across heterogeneous hardware.

  2. J. Urology
    Restriction Spectrum Imaging as a quantitative biomarker for prostate cancer with reliable positive predictive value
    Mariluz Rojo Domingo, Deondre D Do, Christopher C Conlin, and 8 more authors
    The Journal of Urology, 2025
    Abs

    This study establishes the positive predictive value of Restriction Spectrum Imaging (RSI) in identifying clinically significant prostate cancer. By comparing RSI-derived metrics against histopathology, we demonstrate that RSI significantly improves the specificity of MRI-based diagnostics, providing a reliable quantitative framework for clinical decision-making.

2024

  1. medRxiv
    Robustness of a Restriction Spectrum Imaging (RSI) quantitative MRI biomarker for prostate cancer: assessing for systematic bias due to age, race, ethnicity, prostate volume, medication use, or imaging acquisition parameters
    Deondre D Do, Mariluz Rojo Domingo, Christopher C Conlin, and 8 more authors
    medRxiv, 2024
    Abs HTML

    This multi-center study analyzed 1,890 patients across seven institutions to evaluate the robustness of RSIrs as a cancer biomarker. Using multivariate linear mixed-effects models, we demonstrated that the RSIrs metric remains resilient against hardware variations and demographic confounders including age, race, and ethnicity. The findings support RSI as a reliable quantitative tool for large-scale clinical implementation in diverse patient populations.

  2. medRxiv
    Deep learning AI and Restriction Spectrum Imaging for patient-level detection of clinically significant prostate cancer on MRI
    Yuze Song, Mariluz Rojo Domingo, Christopher C Conlin, and 8 more authors
    medRxiv, 2024
    Abs

    We introduce a deep learning architecture integrated with RSI signal modeling to enhance patient-level detection of clinically significant prostate cancer. The hybrid AI-RSI approach yields superior sensitivity compared to conventional PI-RADS scoring, highlighting the potential for automated screening tools in radiology workflows.

  3. IJROBP
    Clinical impact of contouring variability for prostate cancer tumor boost
    Allison Y Zhong, Asona J Lui, Svetlana Kuznetsova, and 8 more authors
    International Journal of Radiation Oncology* Biology* Physics, 2024
    Abs

    Quantifying the variability in tumor contouring among radiation oncologists, this paper explores the subsequent impact on dosage delivery in focal boosts. Our results underscore the need for standardized imaging biomarkers to reduce inter-observer variability and ensure consistent treatment planning.

  4. Sci Reports
    Utility of quantitative measurement of T2 using restriction spectrum imaging for detection of clinically significant prostate cancer
    Mariluz Rojo Domingo, Christopher C Conlin, Roshan Karunamuni, and 8 more authors
    Scientific Reports, 2024
    Abs

    This research investigates the synergy between RSI and quantitative T2 mapping. By isolating specific signal components, we demonstrate that T2-corrected RSI measurements improve the detection of tumors located in the peripheral and transition zones.

  5. medRxiv
    Improved correction of B0 inhomogeneity-induced distortions in diffusion-weighted images of the prostate
    Christopher C Conlin, Aditya Bagrodia, Tristan Barrett, and 8 more authors
    medRxiv, 2024
    Abs

    Geometric distortions caused by B0 inhomogeneity are a major challenge in prostate DWI. We propose an improved correction algorithm that enhances the spatial fidelity of diffusion maps, ensuring that tumor contours align accurately with structural anatomy.

2023

  1. IJROBP
    ReIGNITE radiation therapy boost: a prospective, international study of radiation oncologists’ accuracy in contouring prostate tumors
    Asona J Lui, Karoline Kallis, Allison Y Zhong, and 8 more authors
    International Journal of Radiation Oncology* Biology* Physics, 2023
    Abs

    The ReIGNITE study evaluates the performance of radiation oncologists in contouring tumors using RSI-augmented MRI versus conventional MRI alone. Results show that RSI provides critical spatial cues that increase the accuracy of focal boost volumes.

  2. ISMRM
    Background prostate tissue is quantitatively abnormal on T2-weighted MRI in patients with clinically significant prostate cancer
    Christopher C Conlin, Roshan Karunamuni, Troy S Hussain, and 8 more authors
    In Annual Meeting of ISMRM, 2023
    Abs

    Our findings suggest that the "normal" background tissue in prostates harboring cancer exhibits distinct quantitative signal changes. This ’field effect’ research could lead to new methods for identifying patients at high risk even when visible lesions are ambiguous.
